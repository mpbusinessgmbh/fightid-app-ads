Privacy Policy – Fight-ID

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Fight-ID application handles user information.

Information We Collect

Fight-ID does not collect personal data such as your name, email address, or phone number. The app may process anonymized usage data (for example, which screens are used the most) to improve the user experience.

Advertising

Fight-ID uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Data Storage

No personal data is stored on our own servers. Any data processed by AdMob is handled directly by Google under their own policies.
